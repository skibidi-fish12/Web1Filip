Cheeseburger and Fries Recipe

Ingredients for Cheeseburger

  • 1 lb ground beef (80% lean)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 4 tomato slices
  • Ketchup and mustard (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Ingredients for Fries

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Instructions for Cheeseburger

  1. Form the ground beef into 4 patties, seasoning both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side, until browned and cooked to your desired doneness.
  4. Place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking, allowing it to melt.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns in the skillet for 1-2 minutes until lightly browned.
  6. Assemble the cheeseburgers: place a patty on the bottom half of each bun, then add onion slices, lettuce, and tomato. Top with the other half of the bun.

Instructions for Fries

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Wash and peel the potatoes, then cut them into thin strips or wedges.
  3. Place the potato slices in a bowl, toss with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, and salt.
  4. Spread the fries in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the fries are golden and crispy.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired and serve with the cheeseburgers.
