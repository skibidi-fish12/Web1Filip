Butter Chicken Recipe

Experience the art of Indian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, turmeric, chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight in the fridge for better flavor.
  2. Heat butter in a large pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add the marinated chicken pieces to the pan and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is browned on all sides.
  4. Add the pureed tomatoes to the pan and cook for another 5-10 minutes, until the tomato mixture thickens and the oil starts to separate.
  5. Pour in the cream and water, stirring to combine. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  6. Check seasoning and adjust salt or spices as needed.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
  8. Serve hot with naan or steamed rice.
